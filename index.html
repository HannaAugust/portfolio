REFRACT

Refract is a dynamic collaborative platform where talented students can creatively address organizations' and individuals' needs through civic media micro-projects.

Needs Analysis, Interaction Design, Product Persona Research, User Interface & Navigation, Wireframes & Layout, Prototyping, Visualization, Front-end Development

Process: With Refract, I was involved with the project from inception. From that experience, I learned to work within a team environment at a professional level. I gained experience dealing directly with clients and I learned how to effectively use the revision and iteration process to streamline and troubleshoot individual project components.

Mix

Concept Design, Interactive Design, Virtualization, User Research, User Testing, Prototyping (Proto.io)

Mix gave me a better understanding of the importance of user research. I had to utilize focus groups to give me feedback on how exactly people might use my design. It opened my eyes to how malleable design could be. This experience also let me learn more about prototyping. From the different iterations, I could find my design flaws and troubleshoot more effectively. Because physicality was such a huge component for this project, I had to figure out how to virtualize physical movements, which was a challenge.
