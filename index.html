MVEMJSUN is...
An educational interactive installation utilizing motion capture to inform individuals about the solar system. MVEMJSUN is a digital experience intended to transform a static space into a dynamic environment that is textured with layers of information and ambience.

What is a purpose and takeout?
Beside Informative part of this project, there is also a connection part. We tried to create that connection experience with planets of our solar system, making people feel like they are part of it (which we often forget in every day routine), as well as enjoy this interaction and awake more curiosity. Working with MVEMJSUN gave me hands on experience working with motion-capture for the first time. It also gave me the chance to collect data on how users interact with virtual reality and how to better convey information within the VR experience and create this atmosphere visually.

I did:
Interaction Design, Visual Art, User Experience

Mix.
This interactive piece is an application/framework with interactive virtualization of mixed drinks creation, not just a UI for database of cocktail recipes. Main purpose is to explore mixologist/bartender experience from interactive perspective. Main functionality of application includes interactive virtualization of mixing cocktails, save and share them, obtain more information about them, store them in your own virtual space for later quick access.

Process:
Mix is a personal project, in wich I can demonstrate all my knowledge from design to development. Together with begining user reaserch I was exploring how physical interaction may be simulated virtually. After recearch I desided keep three main functionalities on application: exploration, storage, and interaction, because target audiences' initial response was equally divided. It opened my eyes to how malleable design could be. From testing the different iterations, I could find my design flaws and troubleshoot more effectively.

Skills:
Concept Design, Interactive Design, Virtualization, User Research, User Testing, Prototyping (Proto.io)

Alisa
This is a personal voice assistant, who will guide through and find places of entertainment such as restaurants, bars, cafes and etc. based on interest and preferences of individual at the specific moment. The bot will access the location of the user and ask questions about user’s goal: how far the chosen place should be, how much time the user can spend, what is main agenda, and what kind of service the user looking for (pick up order, hanging out with company, get food, drink, or watch a performance). Based on the input, Alisa will provide with list of possible choices, show the closest rout to the place.

Skills
Interaction Design, Voice Conversational Interface, Information Structure, Prototyping (Sayspring), Design Persona
